# Pre-publish checklist for post: $ARGUMENTS

Run through these checks and report pass ✅ / fail ❌ / needs review ⚠️ for each.

## Structure
- [ ] Main point is clear from the first paragraph
- [ ] Headline is sentence case and earns the click
- [ ] Paragraphs are 2-4 sentences, not walls of text
- [ ] Opening hook is concrete (not a generic observation)
- [ ] Closing has a takeaway or reflection, not just a stop

## Voice
- [ ] No corporate jargon (leverage, synergy, best practices, stakeholders)
- [ ] No excessive adjectives (amazing, incredible, fascinating)
- [ ] No vague intensifiers ("one of the most important", "crucial to understand")
- [ ] No meta-commentary ("as we've explored", "to summarize")
- [ ] Mild profanity: 0-1 uses only, not in headline or opening

## Style (per rules/blog-writing-rules.md)
- [ ] Canadian English spelling (colour, favour, organize, center)
- [ ] Em-dashes: 2 or fewer
- [ ] Lists: varied length, not all threes
- [ ] No "not X, but Y" constructions

## Content
- [ ] No fabricated anecdotes (all personal stories are author-supplied)
- [ ] Dates are specific, not vague ("by Q2", not "soon")
- [ ] Factual claims are accurate or hedged appropriately
- [ ] Code examples (if any) are under 15 lines and focused

## Final
- [ ] Hugo syntax left in place (e.g. qgraph shortcodes)
- [ ] Any `[PERSONAL ANECDOTE: ...]` placeholders filled or removed
- [ ] Ready to publish?

List any failures or warnings with specific line references.
